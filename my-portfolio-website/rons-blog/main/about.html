About Me

This is what I do.

My academic and professional journey has been a testament to adaptability, determination, and a clear vision for growth in the rapidly evolving field of cloud technologies. With a strong educational foundation, I hold a Master’s degree in Communication Engineering, where my research focused on innovative designs in microwave and electromagnetic wave theory. My thesis, titled "Design of Visualization Teaching Hardware for Dynamic Demonstration of Electromagnetic Waves," reflects my ability to bridge theoretical concepts with practical applications. This hands-on approach to hardware design has provided me with a solid technical background that seamlessly complements my transition to cloud engineering.

While my academic pursuits were centred on hardware and communication systems, I have successfully pivoted towards the world of IT and cloud technologies. Recognising the transformative potential of cloud computing, I have actively sought certifications and practical experience to establish myself in this domain. Notably, I earned the AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate (SAA) certification, demonstrating my proficiency in designing scalable, reliable, and cost-efficient cloud solutions on AWS.

My transition is not merely theoretical but grounded in real-world applications. I have undertaken hands-on projects, including the development of a visitor counter application leveraging AWS Amplify, Lambda, DynamoDB, IAM roles, and API Gateway. Through these projects, I have gained practical experience in integrating AWS services to solve complex challenges, showcasing my ability to apply cloud principles effectively.

In addition to my technical skills, my professional experience includes working in IT support, providing technical solutions and marketing strategies, and even developing website solutions for businesses. These experiences have honed my problem-solving abilities, communication skills, and adaptability—qualities that are invaluable in the cloud engineering field.

My journey from hardware-focused roles to cloud technologies is driven by a passion for continuous learning and innovation. My current focus is on gaining further hands-on experience with AWS, deepening my expertise in cloud architecture, and aligning my skills with the demands of the tech industry. As I continue to build my cloud engineering career, I embody the mindset of a lifelong learner, ready to embrace new challenges and make impactful contributions in the field of cloud computing.
